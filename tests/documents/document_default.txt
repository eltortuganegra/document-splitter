“Is that him?”
Cole smashed his cigarette out
in the ashtray of the van. “Yeah.”
I stared at the gray-haired man in the
Gold Viper’s cut, hating him with every fiber
of my being. He was getting onto his chopper
behind Griffin’s, the strip-joint he apparently
owned. “Don’t you dare fucking lose him.”
He frowned. “Relax, I’m not going to lose him.”
